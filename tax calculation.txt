Context
In the Regime Forfettario, income tax (Imposta Sostitutiva) is calculated as a flat rate (e.g., 5% or 15%) on taxable income. Payments are divided into:
1.	Balance Payment (Saldo): The remaining tax due after advance payments for the previous year.
2.	Advance Payments (Acconti): Prepaid taxes for the current year, based on the taxable income of the previous year.
